Methods of Water Conservation


Install water-saving shower heads or flow restrictors

Plant more trees to slow down the suface runoff and replenish underground water

Only run the washing machine and dishwasher when you have a full load.

Canals used for irrigating field should be properly lined to conserve loss of water through seepage

Sprinklers and drip irrigation system are very effective to irrigate the plants and trees without the loss of water through evoporation

Constructing rain water harvesting system can help us conserve water in underground and for our future use,one spell of rain for two hours is enough to save 8,000 litres of water.

Dumping garbages,plastics and other waste materials in to the waterbodies must be avoided

Tighten bolts on ship engines to prevent oil leaks and water pollution

Create awareness among your family and public to reduse the scarcity of water and save it
